About Us

Spark Repair Estimator

Mobile-first repair cost estimator for house flippers.

Our Mission

At Spark, we believe in empowering house flippers with precise and efficient tools to maximize their potential. Our mission is to simplify the complex process of repair cost estimation, providing clear insights and helping you make informed decisions quickly.

What We Do

Spark Repair Estimator is a mobile-first Progressive Web App (PWA) designed specifically for the needs of modern house flippers. It allows you to:

  • Rapidly estimate repair costs on-site.
  • Organize and track multiple projects with ease.
  • Gain insights into potential profits and margins.
  • Generate comprehensive summaries for contractors and partners.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every house flipper, regardless of their experience level, has access to intuitive and powerful tools that streamline their operations and boost their success. Spark is continuously evolving, incorporating user feedback and the latest technological advancements to stay ahead.
